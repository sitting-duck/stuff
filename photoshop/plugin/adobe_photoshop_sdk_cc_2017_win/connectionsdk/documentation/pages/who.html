Who Should Read This?

The is geared towards developers with experience in one or more of the following areas:

  • AIR development on phones or tablets
  • Android development on phones or tablets
  • iOS development on the iPad or iPhone
  • Plug-in developers
  • Java developers
  • Scripting such as JavaScript, AppleScript, or ActionScript

For those who are experienced with Adobe Photoshop but have little exposure to programming, please refer to the Adobe Photoshop Developer Center. For those who do not fall into any of the above groups, please refer to the Requirements section for other listed tutorials, resources, and books to prepare for the .

Developers will learn how to create, develop, and host an application on an iPad or Android tablet and control Photoshop via the new APIs exposed in Photoshop. The tutorials will prepare developers by walking through examples ranging from a simple "Hello World" to more complex examples involving bi-directional communication. Our goal with this SDK is to provide a solid foundation in this new technology through step-by-step tutorials and documentation, then leave it up to the imagination and ingenuity of our developers.
